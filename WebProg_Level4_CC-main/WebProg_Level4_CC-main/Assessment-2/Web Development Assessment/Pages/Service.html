Service Providers

At Bearlot's Furry Friends, we are dedicated to creating meaningful and lasting 'furrytails' that enhance the welfare of cats and dogs.

If you are a Service Provider committed to making a tangible impact in the care and well-being of pets, we invite you to partner with us. Together, we can reach a broader audience, elevate your influence, and drive success in our shared mission of animal welfare.

Why Become Our Partner?

Social Impact

You get to become a part of a bigger cause against animal negligence and animal cruelty. The offers and incentives our users get through you not only encourage but also reward positive actions. Being part of this new ecosystem entails serving the underserved, breaking the stigma, and reshaping mindsets against rescue animals.

Collaborative Events

Collaborating with BFF not only expands your networks but also enhances your participation in special occasions throughout the year. Whether events are initiated by you or other animal enthusiasts within the community, BFF will support marketing efforts to promote these initiatives and contribute to the success of our shared mission.

New Market Potential

The app allows Service Providers to connect with a diverse range of potential customers who may have been inaccessible before. The exclusive discounts BFF users receive from you through the app establish customer loyalty and also act as a catalyst for attracting repeat business, nurturing loyalty through enticing offers.

Community Connectivity

Becoming a part of this new ecosystem links you to a unified platform where the entire animal-loving community, sharing a common mission to protect animals, converges. You get the chance to not only be a spectator of change but instead be involved in making a difference in the pet industry, especially in the rescue world.
