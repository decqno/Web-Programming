About Us

Bearlot’s Furry Friends (BFF) is the UAE’s first digital platform dedicated to supporting animal welfare by bridging the gap between rescue organisations and compassionate individuals. As a team and a platform, we’re committed to helping rescue dogs and cats find safe, loving homes, while empowering organisations with the tools they need to do what they do best. At the heart of BFF is a belief that every animal deserves a chance to live a happy, healthy life and a community that makes that possible.

The Inspiration Behind BFF

Bearlot's Furry Friends was inspired by the experiences of Bear and Merlot, two Chow Chows who witnessed their friend suffer from neglect and confinement. Despite efforts to rescue him, the parent refused, leading to a tragic end. This experience motivated Bear and Merlot's pawrents to create Bearlot's Furry Friends, an initiative dedicated to supporting animal welfare, helping rescues find loving homes, and empowering pawrents to responsibly care for their pets.

Vision

At Bearlot’s Furry Friends, our vision is to redefine pet care and adoption through a platform that empowers every part of the animal welfare ecosystem. We aim to help individuals find their ideal companions, simplify the way pet parents manage care, and support rescue organisations in placing animals into loving homes. Through thoughtful innovation, we’re building a connected, compassionate community where every pet is valued - and where responsible ownership becomes second nature.

Mission

At Bearlot’s Furry Friends, our mission is to transform the pet care experience through an inclusive, tech-driven platform that supports every stage of pet parenthood. We connect animal lovers, rescue organisations, and service providers to streamline adoption and promote responsible ownership. Our app equips pet parents with the tools, resources, and support they need to provide exceptional care. Rooted in a deep commitment to animal welfare, we’re building a connected community where every pet is loved, supported, and given the life they deserve.
