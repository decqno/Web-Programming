Rescue Organization Partnership Program

At Bearlot's Furry Friends, we are passionate about creating furry tails. If you are a Rescue Organization dedicated to making a difference in the welfare of cats and dogs, we want to hear from you!

Why BFF?

Easy Onboarding

We have simplified the onboarding process to ensure you can quickly start using our app. Our team will guide you through every step, making it a smooth transition.

Showcase Your Rescues

BFF allows you to showcase the rescues under your care with stunning profiles, heartwarming stories, and captivating photos, making it easier for potential pawrents to connect with them.

Seamless Communication

BFF facilitates seamless communication between your organization and potential adopters / foster homes, streamlining the process.

Reach a Wider Audience

With our extensive reach, you can connect with potential adopters and foster homes not only in your local area but also from various cities around you.

No Fees

We are dedicated to supporting your mission without any financial burden. We do not charge any fees for onboarding your organization onto our app, nor do we take or process any adoption or fostering fees.

Supporting Rescue Organizations Across UAE

Partner With Us

If you are as passionate as we are about giving rescues a chance for a happy life filled with love, we would be thrilled to have you on board. Together, we can create a world where every rescue finds the perfect forever home.

To join our Rescue Organization Partnership Program, simply fill out the contact form below, and one of our dedicated team members will reach out to you.

Let us work hand in paw to make a difference!
